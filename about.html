Our Mission

Every cricket website tracks batters — run rates, strike rates, centuries, fifties. But what about the bowlers who make or break a match? BowlerStats was built to fill that gap.

We publish over-by-over run analysis for every IPL match. You can see exactly which bowler conceded how many runs in each over, where wickets fell, and which bowlers were most economical under pressure.

Why Bowlers?

Bowlers are the unsung heroes of T20 cricket. A bowler who concedes only 6 runs per over while taking wickets is as valuable as any big-hitting batter. Yet most fans only look at batting scorecards. We believe that changes now.

Our visual histogram shows every over as a bar — the taller the bar, the more expensive the over. Wickets are highlighted in red. You can instantly see which bowler had a nightmare over and which one kept things tight.

Who We Are

BowlerStats is an independent cricket analysis project by fans, for fans. We are not affiliated with the BCCI, IPL, or any cricket board. All data is tracked manually and published as quickly as possible after each match.

We are passionate about cricket statistics and believe that better data leads to better appreciation of the sport. Whether you are a fantasy cricket player, a cricket analyst, or simply a fan who loves going deeper — BowlerStats is for you.

Data Accuracy

We update match data as accurately as possible from live scorecard sources. Our over-by-over figures may occasionally differ slightly from official records due to the manual nature of data entry. If you spot an error, please use our Contact page to let us know.

Advertising

BowlerStats is free to use. We display non-intrusive ads to cover server and hosting costs. We are committed to keeping the user experience clean and fast, especially on mobile devices.
